Optimization Techniques for Digital Rock Physics Workflow


Main Challenges

  • Difficulty to obtain high-quality CT images.
  • Compromise between the field of view (FOV) and the resolution.
  • Sensitivity to the quality of the CT images.
  • Simulation techniques are computationally expensive.

Overview

This project try to optimize Digital Rock Physics (DRP) by tackling challenges in image resolution and segmentation accuracy.

Technical outcomes

  • Demonstrated the use of MONAI, a healthcare AI tool, for DRP problem.
  • Achieved good performance speed up with Data Parallelism.
  • Managed to generate super-resolution images with ESRGAN and diffusion models..
  • Simulated rock properties with ML Algorithm with high accuracy.
